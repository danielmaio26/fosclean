    Desinfection with Ozone

    Ozone is a gas that we find in nature, very useful for its exceptional properties to achieve in-depth hygiene. Ozone is the most powerful natural disinfectant that exists, surpassing the results of bleach or other chlorinated products traditionally used for this purpose thousands of times.

    Needs ranging from obtaining quality air, free of organic and inorganic contamination, as well as bad odors, to purifying drinking water for drinking or for personal hygiene, as well as being very practical for cleaning of the house.

    Nebulization disinfection

    Some viruses and bacteria can remain on surfaces for a long time. Due to the quarantine, many places have been closed and need deep cleaning and disinfection in all corners. The nebulization disinfection technique consists of using a disinfecting liquid, used for operating rooms and clinics, in addition to ultraviolet light. This mist reaches all corners and places of the environment

    • Quick and safe procedure
    • High level of sanitation
    • Microparticles that disinfect places of difficult access
    • Ideal for clinics, offices and beauty salons
    • Can be used in homes and vehicles
